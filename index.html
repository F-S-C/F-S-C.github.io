About Us

Get insights on who we are.

This team was born in 2018 to take a group exam and it's made of five collegues and friends that saw in that exam the oportunity to start working together as a real group. Now we are ready to take any challenge and give life to our ideas.

"FSC" stands for "Five Students of Computer Science": we are five Bachelor's Students of Computer Science and Digital Communication at the University of Bari "Aldo Moro".

The team is composed by:
